    The Number

    A sequence of 10 digits, uniquely put together for the sole purpose of serving you. Sounds mysterious, doesn't it? It really isn't though. It's actually pretty straightforward. The Number is a text message-based delivery system, solely aimed at college campuses. We deliver college students anything - yes, anything, within legal boundaries - from within a certain radius of their campuses, as quick as possible, for very low prices. Text The Number what you want, and where you want it. We will deliver it to you right away, regardless of where you are on campus.
